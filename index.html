Writing Task: Write a short essay (300-400 words) reflecting on a significant milestone in your life.

Guidelines for the Writing Task:
  • Introduction (50-75 words):
    Describe the milestone and its importance. Use the present perfect tense to explain how this milestone has affected you up to now.
    Example: I have achieved a great sense of confidence since graduating from university.
  • Body (150-200 words):
    Detail the events leading up to this milestone. Use the past perfect tense to describe actions or events that were completed before the milestone.
    Example: By the time I graduated, I had completed several internships that prepared me for my career.
  • Conclusion (50-75 words):
    Reflect on the ongoing impact of this milestone using the present perfect tense.
    Example: Since my graduation, I have continued to pursue opportunities that have shaped my professional journey.

Key Requirements:
  • At least 300-400 words total for the essay.
  • Ensure smooth transitions between sections.
